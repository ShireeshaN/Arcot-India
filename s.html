STEP 01 - Contact / Inquiry

You contact us with your request and tell us more about your needs. We help with everything from small to large projects that require documentation and planning. Then we book a visit.

STEP 02 - Visit and Energy Calculation

We visit the site and calculate the energy consumption and lighting requirements based on your space and usage.

STEP 03 - Suggested Solution

We prepare a custom solution with the right fixtures, layout, and smart integration for your space.

STEP 04 - Lighting Calculation

Detailed lighting calculation and layout planning using latest software and photometric data.

STEP 05 - Delivery

Fixtures and accessories are packed and delivered to site based on agreed timeline and project needs.

STEP 06 - Follow-up

Post-delivery support, commissioning, and warranty coverage. We ensure complete satisfaction and performance.
