Transmission

Our transmission solutions ensure seamless, efficient, and reliable power delivery from source to destination. We design and implement high-voltage transmission systems tailored to meet industrial and urban needs with precision engineering.

High Voltage Transmission Lines (66kV to 765kV)

End-to-end solutions for EHV transmission systems, ensuring maximum efficiency and minimal losses.

Substation Design & Execution

Comprehensive services for both greenfield and brownfield substations, optimized for safety and scalability.

Overhead & Underground Transmission

Proven capabilities in both conventional overhead and space-constrained underground cable transmission systems.

Transmission Tower Erection & Stringing

Precision-driven erection and stringing of towers, even in challenging geographical conditions.

GIS & AIS Substations

Design and execution of both Gas Insulated and Air Insulated substations tailored to project needs.

Turnkey EPC Solutions

Full-spectrum Engineering, Procurement, and Construction services for seamless project delivery.

Statcom and Reactive Power Systems

Advanced systems for grid stability, reactive power management, and voltage control.
